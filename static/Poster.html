Goal 2: Convert

Sighted braille learners can check their accuracy while learning.

Parents/Loved ones and educators are the primary intended audience for this section.
Individuals experiencing slow-onset sight loss may also benefit from using it.

Strategies for Dot Detection: Light-based. Dual approach (shadows and peaks). Tiling and reassembly for inconsistent lighting.

Strategies for Dot Identification and Cell Detection: UEB standardized dimensions. Average dot diameter. Average distance between adjacent dots.

Conversion Process Photos

  1. Original photo: grade1 UEB saying “re be ligh” (segment of the phrase let there be light).
  2. After Dot Detection: same photo, but with green pixels for peaks and black dots for shadows.
  3. After Cell Detection: cells = [[1, 2, 3, 5], [1, 5], 0, [1, 2, 3]. [2, 4], [1, 2, 4, 5], [1, 2, 5]]
  4. Output Text: TEXT = re be ligh
